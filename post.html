All tourism shares some important characteristics: Intangibility: you can't hold tourism. Perishability: an unsold plane seat is a lost opportunity to make money. Seasonality: customers may be more or less likely to go somewhere with changing seasons.

Tourism is the act of traveling away from home to relax, have fun, or pursue recreation. It is a major contributor to the economies of many countries.
